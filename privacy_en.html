Privacy Policy

* This document is a translation of the Japanese Privacy Policy. In the event of any discrepancy between the Japanese version and this translated version, the Japanese version shall prevail.

"move_master" (hereinafter referred to as the "App") prioritizes the protection of user privacy and establishes the following policy.

1. Data Collection and Use

The App uses the device's camera to analyze video (skeleton detection, comparative playback, etc.) for the purpose of assisting dance practice. All of these processes are performed internally (locally) on your device.

Important Notice: Your video data, analyzed skeleton data, and any personally identifiable information are NEVER transmitted to or stored on the developer's or any external servers.

2. Use of Permissions

The App requires the following permissions:

  • Camera: For recording videos and real-time analysis.
  • Photo Library / Storage: For loading user-selected videos and saving recorded videos.
  • Microphone: For recording audio during video capture (only when necessary).
