2. Tips for Successful AI Analysis

To maintain accuracy, please note the following:

  • Prevent overlapping: Film in a place where you do not overlap with others.
  • Stay in frame: Ensure your body does not go out of the camera angle during movements.
  • Mirror reflections: Minimize reflections in mirrors to prevent AI misdetection.
