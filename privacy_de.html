Privacy Policy

MoveMaster (hereinafter 'the App') prioritizes user privacy and has established the following policy.

1. Data Collection and Use

The App analyzes video using the device camera locally on the device to assist with dance practice.

Important Notice: User video data, music data, analyzed skeleton data, and personal identification information are NEVER sent to or saved on external servers.

2. Use of Permissions

The App requires the following permissions:

  • Camera: For shooting video and real-time analysis.
  • Photo Library/Storage: To load selected videos/music and save recorded videos.
  • Microphone: For audio recording during video capture (only when needed).
